Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Note Pad Pro. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application handles user data. By using this app, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Data Collection

Note Pad Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes, passwords, and backups are stored locally on your device only. We do not use any external servers to store user content.

3. Permissions We Use

The app may request the following permissions to enable specific features:

  • Notifications: Used for reminders, sticky notes, and alerts.
  • Storage / Media Access: Used to create backups, restore notes, and export PDFs or images.
  • Overlay (Draw over other apps): Used for the Floating Note feature.
  • Biometric: Used to lock and unlock the app securely. Biometric data is never stored by us.
  • Camera: Used only for OCR (Image-to-Text) and QR code scanning features.

4. Advertisements

Note Pad Pro may display ads provided by third-party advertising services (such as Start.io). These services may collect anonymous usage data to improve ad performance. We do not share personal information with advertisers.

5. Cloud & Backup Features

When using backup or cloud-sharing features, the app allows you to export files (such as JSON backups) and share them using your chosen apps or storage services. We do not control or access these external services.

6. Data Security

We use system-level security features such as app lock and biometric authentication to protect your data. However, you are responsible for keeping your device secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Note Pad Pro is safe for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
