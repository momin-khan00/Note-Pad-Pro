Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: DECEMBER 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Note Pad Pro. Your privacy is not just a policy; it's our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. Unlike other apps, we believe your notes belong only to you.

Data Collection

We believe in the "Zero-Knowledge" principle.

  • Local Storage: All your notes are stored locally on your device's internal database (SQLite).
  • No Cloud Uploads: We do not upload your notes to any external server.
  • Backup Files: If you use the Backup feature, the JSON file is stored strictly on your device storage.

Permissions We Ask

To provide advanced features, we need specific access:

  • Biometric: To lock/unlock the app using your Fingerprint/Lock.
  • Notifications: To show Sticky Notes in your status bar.
  • Storage: To save PDF exports and Backup files.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption for the Biometric Lock feature. However, please note that since data is stored on your device, ensuring your device's security (screen lock) is your responsibility.
